Once shed, we view them as passive beings (waste / resource / evidence)

protective when attached

adaptive when challenged

regenrative when lost

Shed Hair Archive is a collective experiment investigating shed hair as an extension of identity through the lens of auto-ethnography and epigeneitcs.
